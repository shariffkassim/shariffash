How It Started

On the 25th of July, 2023, the small town of Newford witnessed the grand opening of Kassim's Pharmaceutical Company. The brainchild of Shariff Kassim, a seasoned pharmacist with a vision for accessible and affordable healthcare, the company began its journey in a modest building on Elm Street. Shariff's passion for medicine was kindled in his early years, watching his father, a renowned herbalist, treat the sick with natural remedies. This inspired Shariff to pursue a formal education in pharmacy, blending traditional knowledge with modern science. After years of working for various pharmaceutical giants, he decided it was time to carve his own path. Kassim's Pharmaceutical Company was not just another business venture; it was Shariff's commitment to his community. He wanted to ensure that everyone, regardless of their financial status, had access to essential medications. To this end, he negotiated with suppliers to get the best rates, kept his profit margins low, and set up a fund to assist those who couldn't afford even the minimal costs...
